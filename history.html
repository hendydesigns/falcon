H I S T O R Y

A Humble Start

The history of Falcon Construction is one of perseverance and humble beginnings. Wieslaw “Kaz” Malachowski arrived in the United States in 1982, fleeing oppressive conditions in then-communist Poland and turning aside his studies at the Theatrical Academy of Warsaw. On his first night in a foreign land, Kaz’s friends insisted on celebrating his arrival even though they had to be up at dawn to paint the exterior of a home. Despite the jet lag, culture shock, and lack of sleep, Kaz was the only one painting the exterior at 7AM the next day.

Foundation in Exellence

This story encapsulates the work ethic and dedication that became our motto: “Perfectionists in Workmanship.” Kaz’s dedication to perfection led to Falcon Construction being founded as a sole proprietorship in 1984 and later incorporated in Maryland in 1989. Since then, we have grown from humble beginnings to a General Contractor renowned in the Washington, D.C. area.

Continuing the Legacy

We are a general contractor and remodeler known for making in-house built-ins and cabinets as well as having established excellent working relations with the most renowned architectural firms in the Washington Metropolitan area, and having thus developed a solid and very satisfied customer base.
